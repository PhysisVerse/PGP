## Summary

This proposal seeks approval to allocate a continuation grant of **10T USDC** to **Physis Labs** for **Activation P2**, extending the Activation Phase through the **start of February**. This tranche continues execution from Activation P1, with emphasis on stabilization, operator retention, and conversion of early activity into sustained network usage.

---

## Impact

* **Why Now:**

  * Activation P1 focuses on initial beta onboarding and live operation.
  * Activation P2 consolidates those gains into stable, repeatable processes ahead of a wider public growth window.

* **Opportunities:**

  * Improve operator experience and reduce churn.
  * Validate uptime, monitoring, and update cycles under sustained use.
  * Transition from initial activation to predictable operations.

* **Challenges:**

  * Early-stage networks require careful tuning to avoid fatigue or instability.
  * Scope discipline is required to avoid premature expansion.

* **Future Vision:**

  * A successful Activation P2 positions the ecosystem for broader scale initiatives and structured public growth phases in 2026.

## Explanation

* **Implementation Overview:**

  * DAO will allocate **10T USDC** to Physis Labs to support:

    * Continued SmartSpot operator support and retention
    * Network performance tuning and reliability improvements
    * Refinement of onboarding and update workflows
    * Ongoing sourcing and logistics coordination for SmartSpot Thor

* **Practical Examples:**

  * Iterative improvements based on live operator usage patterns.
  * Deployment of stability-focused updates.
  * Reduction of onboarding friction identified in Activation P1.

* **Addressing Corner Cases:**

  * Any unused funds will be carried forward or returned to Treasury subject to Council review.
